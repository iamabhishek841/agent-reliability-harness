# Refund Window Exceptions

Policy owner: Customer Operations. Version 2025.1. Effective 2025-01-01.

The standard return window is 30 calendar days after confirmed delivery. Human reviewers may extend this window for a documented carrier delay, natural disaster, accessibility accommodation, active-duty deployment, or an acknowledged company service incident. The evidence for an exception must be attached to the case. Agents may explain that an exception review is available, but automated agents must not grant the exception.

Requests received outside the standard window are denied when the delivery timestamp is reliable and no qualifying exception is present. If the customer alleges an exception, the correct outcome is escalation rather than approval or categorical denial. Marketing promises and informal chat statements do not supersede this policy unless linked to an approved campaign identifier.
