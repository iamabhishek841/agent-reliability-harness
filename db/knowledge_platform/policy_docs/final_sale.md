# Final-Sale and Non-Returnable Items

Policy owner: Merchandising Risk. Version 2025.1. Effective 2025-01-01.

Items marked final_sale in the order system are not eligible for an automated refund. The restriction includes clearance inventory, personalized goods, downloadable products after access, hygiene-sensitive goods after opening, gift cards, and products whose resale is legally restricted. The product flag in the commerce record is authoritative; customer-supplied descriptions are not.

A final-sale order may be reviewed by a human when the customer reports damage, misdescription, counterfeit goods, a safety defect, or a statutory consumer-right claim. The agent must preserve the final-sale flag in the trace and route the case to the appropriate review queue. It must not remove or reinterpret the flag to reach an approval.
