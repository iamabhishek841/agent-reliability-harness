# Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Goods

Policy owner: Customer Care. Version 2025.1. Effective 2025-01-01.

Damage, defect, wrong-item, and materially-not-as-described claims are eligible for specialist review even when a final-sale restriction or standard-window issue exists. The claimant should provide a description and, where reasonable, photographs or a carrier damage report. Absence of images must not automatically defeat a statutory claim.

The automated agent may approve a routine in-window refund only when no exclusion flag applies and the normal evidence is complete. It must escalate safety issues, regulated products, repeated damage claims, high-value goods, or conflicts between warehouse and customer records. The trace should record that the claim category affected routing without treating an unverified allegation as fact.
